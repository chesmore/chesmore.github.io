About

I am a PhD Candidate and National Science Foundation Graduate Student Research Fellow at the University of Chicago. I work with Professor Jeff McMahon as part of The Simons Observatory, Advanced ACTPol, and the CMB-S4 collaborations. In my research, I measure and model the optical systematics of The Simons Observatory Large Aperture Telescope. This involves software development to model complex optical systems such as the Simons Observatory Large Aperture Telescope, and also preparing experiments to test optical systematics, involving mm-wave electronics and signal processing.
